FAME's Curriculum

At FAME, we understand that every child learns and grows at their own pace. That's why our curriculum is designed to offer a unique and engaging journey for students from Nursery through prep 6. Our dedicated teachers foster a love of learning through developmentally-appropriate activities that address the mind, body, and spirit.

  • Rooted in Christian Values

    FAME incorporates core Christian values throughout the curriculum, nurturing kindness, respect, and compassion. By integrating age-appropriate Bible stories, inspirational songs, and thoughtful discussions, we encourage students to explore their faith in a supportive environment.

  • Empowering All Learners

    FAME celebrates diversity in learning styles and abilities. Our smaller class sizes allow for personalized attention, and our teachers utilize a variety of engaging instructional methods. This includes hands-on activities, differentiated instruction, and positive reinforcement to ensure each student reaches their full potential.

Nursery Curriculum

FAME Nursery School

Recognizes the profound impact of early childhood experiences on a child's lifelong development. Our curriculum fosters a love of learning through a play-based approach that cultivates curiosity, creativity, and essential skills in a nurturing Christian environment.

The Power of Play

  • Engaging Learning Centers: Our thoughtfully designed indoor and outdoor learning centers provide a variety of stimulating environments that cater to diverse learning styles and interests. These centers may include block building areas, dramatic play spaces, art studios, sensory exploration areas, and inviting reading nooks.

  • Teacher-Facilitated Exploration: Experienced teachers curate engaging themes and learning objectives around these play experiences. This ensures a balance between child-initiated exploration and purposeful learning, maximizing the developmental benefits of play.

Benefits of Our Playful Curriculum

  1. Cognitive Development: Playful activities stimulate language acquisition, early literacy and numeracy skills, and critical thinking abilities.

  2. Social-Emotional Development: Play provides opportunities for children to develop positive social interactions, collaboration skills, and effective communication. Children learn to share, take turns, and resolve conflicts in a supportive environment.

  3. Physical Development: Active play promotes healthy growth and development of gross and fine motor skills.

  4. Creative Expression: Through art activities, storytelling, and dramatic play, children explore their creativity and imagination.

A Holistic Approach

Beyond the core benefits of play, FAME Nursery School offers a holistic approach to early childhood education:
  • Social-Emotional Learning: We integrate activities that encourage emotional intelligence, self-awareness, and healthy ways to express feelings.

  • Spiritual Growth: We foster good Christian morals and Spiritual growth by incorporating age-appropriate Bible stories and songs in our daily activities.

  • Healthy Habits: We promote healthy habits like handwashing, healthy eating, and getting enough sleep to support overall well-being.

Investing in Your Child's Future

By choosing FAME Nursery School, you're investing in your child's future. Our nurturing and stimulating environment fosters a love of learning and supports the development of essential skills that will benefit them throughout their academic journey. Dedicated and experienced teachers ensure each child feels loved, respected, and valued.

Navigate the Path to Academic Excellence! Develop and master success habits for JSS and beyond.

Prep 4-6 Curriculum

FAME Prep 4-6

Serves as a pivotal bridge between primary and secondary education. Our program goes beyond simply preparing students for the National Primary School Examination (NPSE). We cultivate a learning environment that ignites intellectual curiosity, fosters critical thinking skills, and equips students with the academic tools and confidence needed to thrive in Junior Secondary School and beyond.

  • Mastering Language Across Diverse Landscapes: The curriculum in Prep 4-6 places a strong emphasis on language proficiency. We move beyond rote memorization to develop a comprehensive skillset that allows students to effectively use language in various contexts. This includes honing their listening, speaking, reading, writing, viewing, and representing skills. Students engage with a range of multicultural and multimedia resources, developing a strong understanding of information and ideas regardless of its source.

  • Nurturing Critical Thinkers: FAME goes beyond spoon-feeding facts. We actively engage students in the learning process, encouraging them to analyze information, ask questions, and form their own conclusions. This approach fosters critical thinking skills, allowing students to become independent learners who are confident in their ability to solve problems and evaluate situations thoughtfully.

  • Building the Pillars of Academic Success: Transitioning to Junior Secondary School demands increased independence and academic responsibility. At FAME, we equip students with the tools they need to excel in this new environment. We emphasize strong organizational skills, teaching them effective study habits and time management techniques. This sets the stage for them to become self-directed learners who are accountable for their own academic success.

  • Building a Faith Foundation: Our program fosters a welcoming and inclusive environment where students can explore their faith in a safe and supportive setting. Teachers act as role models of Christian values, demonstrating the importance of faith in their interactions with students.

  • Integration with Core Values: Spiritual growth is not a separate subject at FAME. Christian values are woven into the fabric of our entire curriculum, fostering a sense of purpose and compassion in all aspects of learning.
